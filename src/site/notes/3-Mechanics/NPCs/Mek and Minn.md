After [[1-Party/The Dream Team/The Dream Team\|The Dream Team]] defeated him, they moved to [[2-World/Clifftop Observatory\|Clifftop Observatory]] and reconciled with [[3-Mechanics/NPCs/Myla\|Myla]].
